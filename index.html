ABOUT IPANEMAVIBE

Ipanema VIBE is a new, exciting and innovative printed, digital and application magazine, all in English, created to inform foreign visitors to Rio about everything that is “Ipanema,” has to offer.

Ipanema VIBE magazine will have 40 glossy high quality pages, intelligent and creative contents, interviews and other interesting subjects to feed the expectations of all kinds of readers.

Ipanema VIBE will raise businesses in the area to international standards and provoke very positive changes.

Its 0 Edition will be launched just before Rio carnival 2017. There will be free 10.000 copies distributed complimentary and available at the most influential establishments in Ipanema.

Why vibe

Our aim is to raise the profile and excellence of local businesses in the area to international standards, stimulating growth and positive change, benefitting not only our foreign visitors, but also our unique and beloved city.

Our credibility will be based on offering quality reading, great looking magazine and reliable reviews.

This is the first and only high quality printed magazine on the Brazilian market and it is with great pleasure that we share it with you and our new readers!

Who we are

The Ipanema VIBE team is a group of entrepreneurs and professionals, all with specific knowledge about various aspects of Ipanema, who have come together with the aim and desire to put together a high end and informative media publication which will make a difference!

It is our belief that our shared collective knowledge and passion for Ipanema has the capability to create a special and unique experience for our visitors.

Ipanema is our place of residence and social interaction. Many friends from abroad have experienced our more personal guidance and we believe that with Ipanema VIBE we will be able to reach out to a much wider range of foreigners in Rio who, so far, had limited source of reliable guides.
